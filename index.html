What is the Latin pig?

In Pig Latin, you take the first consonant of the English word and put it at the end and then add -ay. For example, "What a beautiful day!" translates to "Atwhay ayay eautifulbay ayda!". For words that begin with a vowel or a silent letter, simply add -way to the end of the word
